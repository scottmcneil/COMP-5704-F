Project Title: Speeding Up Wild Clustered Bootstrap with Batched GEMMs on GPUs

Name: Scott McNeil

Project Outline:

The objective for this project is to use parallel computing to optimize the Wild Cluster Bootstrap, due to Cameron, Gelbach and Miller (2008).

Specifically, the goal is to reformulate the problem as a series of batched General Matrix to Matrix Multiplications (GEMMS), due to work done by Masliah et. al. (2016) and Jhurani and Mullowney (2015). For benchmarking and comparison, the paper will rely on a series of implementations and conceptualizations, including Giacomini, Politis and White (2013), Roodman (2015) and Neto (2015).

  • Jhurani and Mullowney (2015)
